Features lab

Project delivered during my work at Linx. The company Linx provides personalized customers' experiences in e-commerce. I had the challenge to evolve the current releases page. To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted confidential information in this case study.

Discover

Since the acquisition in 2015, the number of new features released has increased, and the users did not have visibility about the evolution of the products.

To better understand the problem we've analyzed NPS results, interviewed some users, and performed a competitive analysis.

Connect

The data collected was properly documented to facilitate the analysis of results, such as identifying patterns among responses.
